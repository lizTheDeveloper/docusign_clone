## üìä Strategic Prioritization Framework Used

### Primary Frameworks Applied:
1. **RICE Scoring** - Reach √ó Impact √ó Confidence / Effort
2. **Value Chain Mapping** - Porter's Value Chain for competitive advantage
3. **VRIO Analysis** - Valuable, Rare, Inimitable, Organized capabilities
4. **Kano Model** - Basic expectations vs. performance vs. delighters
5. **Jobs To Be Done** - Core job = "sign documents legally and efficiently"

### Business Value Dimensions:
- **Revenue Impact** (40% weight) - Direct revenue generation, ARPU increase, or market expansion
- **Strategic Positioning** (30% weight) - Competitive differentiation, moat building, compliance
- **Operational Efficiency** (20% weight) - Support cost reduction, automation, user self-service
- **User Satisfaction** (10% weight) - NPS drivers, delight factors, viral loops

### Dependencies Considered:
- Technical dependencies (e.g., auth before RBAC)
- Market dependencies (e.g., compliance before enterprise sales)
- Resource constraints (parallel development streams)
